Point Spread (Handicap) Betting

What is Point Spread (Handicap) Betting?

Point Spread betting, also known as Handicap betting, levels the playing field by giving an advantage or disadvantage to a team based on their expected performance. You bet on whether a team will win or lose by a specified margin.

Tips for Winning Point Spread Bets:

  • Understand how the spread affects the outcome of your bet.
  • Analyze past performance and team consistency against the spread.
  • Look for matches where the spread seems favorable based on team strengths.

When to Use:

  • When one team is significantly stronger but odds on a straight win are low.
  • When a weaker team is expected to perform better than expected.

When to Avoid:

  • Matches with unpredictable outcomes or inconsistent team performance.
  • Games involving teams with significant injuries or other uncertainties.
