Asian Handicap Betting

What is Asian Handicap Betting?

Asian Handicap betting levels the playing field by giving one team a virtual advantage or disadvantage. It eliminates the possibility of a draw, meaning bets are refunded if the adjusted score ends in a tie.

Tips for Winning Asian Handicap Bets:

  • Understand the handicap system and how it adjusts scores.
  • Analyze the form and strength of both teams carefully.
  • Use Asian Handicaps when there's a clear difference in team strength but odds for a direct win are unattractive.

When to Use:

  • When you expect a close match and want to cover potential outcomes.
  • For matches involving heavy favorites with low odds for a direct win.

When to Avoid:

  • If you're unfamiliar with how Asian Handicaps work.
  • When betting on matches with unpredictable teams or outcomes.
