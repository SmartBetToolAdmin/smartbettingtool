What is Time of Goal Betting?

Time of Goal betting involves predicting the time range when a goal will be scored during a match. These ranges can vary, such as the first 10 minutes, 15 minutes, or by each half.

  • Consider match importance; crucial matches may have early goals as teams seek dominance.
  • Review head-to-head statistics and past patterns of goal timings.

When to Use:

  • Matches with a clear favorite expected to dominate early.
  • Games involving teams known for early attacking strategies.

When to Avoid:

  • Matches involving defensive teams that tend to score late or not at all.
  • Games where both teams are cautious and prioritize defense over attack.
  • Matches in bad weather conditions, which may delay scoring opportunities.
