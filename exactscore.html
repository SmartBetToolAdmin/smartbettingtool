Exact Score Betting

What is Exact Score Betting?

Exact Score betting involves predicting the precise final score of a match. This bet type can offer high payouts due to its challenging nature.

Tips for Winning Exact Score Bets:

  • Analyze team scoring trends and defensive strengths.
  • Consider historical head-to-head scorelines.
  • Look for games involving teams with consistent scoring patterns.

When to Use:

  • For games with predictable scorelines, such as matches involving strong defenses.
  • When betting on games between high-scoring teams with a predictable margin of victory.

When to Avoid:

  • Games between unpredictable teams with erratic performances.
  • Matches involving high stakes, where results can be highly volatile.
