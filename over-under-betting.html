Over/Under Betting Tips

Over/under betting allows you to predict the total number of goals in a match. Here are some tips:

  • Analyze average goals scored and conceded by both teams.
  • Consider games in high-scoring leagues for over 2.5 goals bets.
  • Look for defensive teams in low-scoring leagues for under 2.5 goals bets.
  • Factor in match importance; critical matches often have fewer goals.
