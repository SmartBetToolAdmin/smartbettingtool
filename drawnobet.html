Draw No Bet Betting

What is Draw No Bet Betting?

Draw No Bet removes the draw option from a match. If the game ends in a draw, your stake is refunded. You win if your chosen team wins.

Tips for Winning Draw No Bet Bets:

  • Choose matches where one team has a slight edge but a draw is still possible.
  • Look for good odds on teams with strong home records.
  • Consider using this bet when you're confident in a team but want some insurance.

When to Use:

  • In matches where teams are evenly matched but you favor one slightly.
  • When you want to mitigate risk in potentially low-scoring games.

When to Avoid:

  • Games where one team is a clear favorite and the odds for Draw No Bet are too low.
  • Matches involving unpredictable teams with inconsistent performances.
